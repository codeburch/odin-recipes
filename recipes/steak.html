Steak

This is my favorite dish, a nice juicy and medium rare Steak.

You don't want to cook the steak for too long as it will lose its juiciness.

Ingredients

  • High Quality Cut Meat
  • Basil
  • Steak Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season pan.
  2. Turn stove on to medium heat and let pan get warm.
  3. Sear steak on each side and then let steak sit for a few minutes.
  4. Flip steak.
  5. Enjoy!
